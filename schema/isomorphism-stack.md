### Overall layout

- **Canvas:** 1600×1200 px, white background  
- **Title (top center):**  
  - Text: `SUBIT Isomorphism Stack (12 Canonical Layers)`  
  - Font: Sans‑serif, 32 px, bold, #111111  
- **Structure:** 12 horizontal bands (layers), stacked vertically, all same height  
- **Margins:** 80 px left/right, 100 px top, 80 px bottom  
- **Band height:** ~70–75 px each, with 20 px vertical spacing

---

### Common band style

- **Band background:** alternating light greys  
  - Even layers: #F7F7F7  
  - Odd layers: #ECECEC  
- **Band border:** #DDDDDD, 1 px  
- **Left label column:** fixed width 260 px  
- **Right content area:** remaining width

Inside each band:

- **Layer index:** small tag at far left, e.g. `L1`, `L2`  
  - Font: 14 px, bold, #666666  
- **Layer name:** to the right of index  
  - Font: 18 px, bold, #222222  
- **Formal expression:** on same line or second line, monospaced  
  - Font: 16 px, monospace, #333333  

---

### Layer definitions

**L1 — Binary Representation**  
- Label: `L1  Binary Representation`  
- Expression: `{0,1}⁶`  
- Subtext (smaller, optional): `Six independent binary axes.`

**L2 — Polarity Representation**  
- Label: `L2  Polarity Representation`  
- Expression: `{Yin, Yang}⁶`  
- Subtext: `Two-pole mapping of the six bits.`

**L3 — Energetic Polarity Representation**  
- Label: `L3  Energetic Polarity Representation`  
- Expression: `{Anima, Animus}⁶`  
- Subtext: `Equivalent two-pole system.`

**L4 — Structural Coordinate Triplet**  
- Label: `L4  Structural Coordinate Triplet`  
- Expression: `{WHO, WHERE, WHEN}`  
- Subtext: `Three orthogonal axes.`

**L5 — Scale Triplet**  
- Label: `L5  Scale Triplet`  
- Expression: `{MICRO, MACRO, META}`  
- Subtext: `Three independent scale dimensions.`

**L6 — Bigram Representation (Binary)**  
- Label: `L6  Bigram Representation (Binary)`  
- Expression: `{10, 11, 01, 00}³`  
- Subtext: `Three bigrams from four possible states.`

**L7 — Bigram Representation (Abstract)**  
- Label: `L7  Bigram Representation (Abstract)`  
- Expression: `{4 bigrams}³`  
- Subtext: `Abstract form of the bigram layer.`

**L8 — Subject–Vector–Phase Triplet (Abstract)**  
- Label: `L8  Subject–Vector–Phase Triplet (Abstract)`  
- Expression: `{4 subjects, 4 vectors, 4 phases}`  
- Subtext: `Three sets of four elements.`

**L9 — Subject–Vector–Phase Triplet (Concrete)**  
- Label: `L9  Subject–Vector–Phase Triplet (Concrete)`  
- Expression (multiline, monospace):  
  - Line 1: `ME / WE / YOU / THEY`  
  - Line 2: `EAST / SOUTH / WEST / NORTH`  
  - Line 3: `SPRING / SUMMER / AUTUMN / WINTER`

**L10 — Trigram Representation (Abstract)**  
- Label: `L10  Trigram Representation (Abstract)`  
- Expression: `{8 trigrams}²`  
- Subtext: `Two trigrams from the canonical set of eight.`

**L11 — Trigram Representation (Canonical Order)**  
- Label: `L11  Trigram Representation (Canonical Order)`  
- Expression (monospace, wrap if needed):  
  - `{100,101,110,111,011,010,001,000}²`

**L12 — Color Representation**  
- Label: `L12  Color Representation (Canonical Palette)`  
- Expression: `{Blue, Green, Lime, Yellow, Orange, Red, Burgundy, Purple}²`  
- On the right side of this band, add **8 small color swatches** in a row:  
  - Size: 24×24 px squares  
  - Colors (left→right): Blue, Green, Lime, Yellow, Orange, Red, Burgundy, Purple  
  - Each with 4 px spacing
